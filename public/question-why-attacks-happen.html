Why do shark attacks happen?

There are two types of shark attacks: provoked attacks and unprovoked attacks. Data shows that most provoked attacks occur when a human invades a sharks habitat and space or bothers its space and natural habits. Unprovoked attacks are when a shark attacks a human without a specific reason. Unprovoked attacks are very rarely done with malicious intent, like many shark movies will have you believe. Sometimes sharks think a surfer or person on a boat is prey and attacks them, then swims away once they realize their mistake.
