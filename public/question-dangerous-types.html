What types of sharks are dangerous?

With the statistics below, we now know which sharks to potentially avoid. We know Great White Sharks cause a lot of attacks on humans, but also that only 25% of those attacks are fatal. Although attacks are common, most of them are most likely provoked or accidental.

"Great" White Shark

37% of attacks on humans are from Great White Sharks
22% of Great White Shark attacks are fatal

Tiger Shark

15% of attacks on humans are from Tiger Sharks
26% of Tiger Shark attacks are fatal

Bull Shark

10% of attacks on humans are from Bull Sharks
21% of Bull Shark attacks are fatal

Nurse Shark

5% of attacks on humans are from Nurse Sharks
1% of Nurse Shark attacks are fatal

Reef Shark

4% of attacks on humans are from Reef Sharks
0% of Reef Sharks attacks are fatal

Whaler Shark

3% of attacks on humans are from Whaler Sharks
7% of Whaler Shark attacks are fatal
